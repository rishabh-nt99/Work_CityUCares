Our Mascots

Every year Counselling Service selects a new mascot for the CityU Cares for You Campaign, and announces what the new mascot is in the campaign Kick-off Ceremony in semester A.

It is our tradition to disseminate mascot-shaped plush toys/stress balls, fresh fruits and other souvenirs during the campaign to spread our caring messages to fellow students and staff.
Past Mascots:

1. King Duck (2016/2017)
2. Owl (2017/2018)
3. Vestley, Flying Pig (2018/2019)
2019/2020 Mascot? Stay tuned!
You can also visit our mascot in Student Development Services General Office!
