Semester A 18/19

Date: 19 – 22 November 2018
Theme: Mutual Support
Activities:

  • Graduation and Inauguration Ceremony of Caring League: Recognize the contribution of 17/18 Caring Leaders and welcome the new Caring Leaders of this year aboard.

  • Figure Out Your Love Languages: Find out what love you and your significant others desire!

  • “Hea” Zone: Sit down, get a candy, and take a little break from your busy life!

    Semester B 18/19

    Date: 8 – 12 April 2019
    Theme: Mutual Support
    Activities:

  • Helping Skills Exhibition: Equip yourself with helping skills and support the others in the best way!

  • Peer Support Corner: Organized by Peer Counsellor, Caring League; Share your feelings by writing letters to Peer Counsellors, or sticking your message on the Tree of Hope

  • Positivity Bookmark: organized by Positivity Ambassador, Caring League; Bookmarks with positive quotes designed by Caring Leaders are disseminated.

  • Popcorn Station: Free freshly made popcorn are distributed to students and staff

  • Busking performance: Flow in the sea of music and let the stress go.

  • Positive Psychology Exhibition: organized by Positivity Ambassador, Caring League; Share life tips to fellow schoolmate to embrace a positive mind

    Semester A 17/18

    Date: 13 – 23 November 2017
    Theme: You’ll never walk alone
    Activities:

  • Comforting Corner: Shake a bamboo stick from the cylinder and get those stunning yet meaningful cheering cards!

  • Photo Booth: Organized by City mover, Caring League; Bring along your friend and record the moment of friendship with an instant photo!

  • Graduation and Inauguration Ceremony of Caring League: Show gratitude to 2016/2017 Caring Leaders for their contribution in establishing a caring campus, and welcome 2017/2018 Caring Leaders to the Caring League family.

    Semester B 17/18

    Date: 9 – 12 April 2018
    Theme: Take a Break and Walk Further
    Activities:

  • Cafe Station: Free freshly brewed coffee are distributed to students and staff

  • Ping-Ping Pong-Pong: organized by City Mover, Caring League; An extraordinary table-tennis game which played with things like flip flops and notebook instead of traditional rackets!

  • Galaxy Jar Workshop: organized by Inclusion Ambassador, Caring League; By simply adding and mixing the ingredients, you can create your own galaxy!

  • Positive Psychology Exhibition: organized by Positivity Ambassador, Caring League; Share life tips to fellow schoolmate to embrace a positive mind
