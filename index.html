CityU Cares For You

Background:

Sometimes things are too hard and life seems unbearable. Tensions from study, family, relationship…all these make you feel suffocated and powerless to handle. We understand, and we want to tell you that you are not alone.


We believe that having someone’s support is the key in overcoming adversities. CityU Cares for You Campaign has been launched since 2015. It usually takes place in the 10 th / 11 th week of every semester, which is the period that students are stressed by loads of assignments and examinations preparation. So in such a tough time we would like to express our care and support to every corner in CityU, and to show our concern on students’ well-being.


Through the campaign, we aim to:
  • Promote positive attitude
  • Cultivate mutual support among students
  • Strengthen students’ resilience to overcome personal challenges
  • Reinforce university-students relationship
